Who We Are

Knoxville's Natural Stone Fabricator

Years of expertise await you when you choose Stonecraft. Over 40 years of extensive knowledge working with natural stone is what sets us apart. The rainbow is your palette and the limits are boundless as our knowledgeable sales staff helps you create the exact look you want.

What We Work With

Materials

Every slab in our yard is handpicked. We carry the full spectrum of natural and engineered stone for any application.
01
Granite

The oldest building material on earth, quarried since 4,000 B.C. A unique grain flow, striking crystalline structure, and beautiful durability — ideal for kitchen countertops and high-traffic surfaces.

Most Popular
02
Marble

Brilliantly reflects light with a natural warmth that no engineered surface can replicate. Timeless for bathroom vanities, fireplace surrounds, and furniture tops. Each piece entirely one-of-a-kind.

03
Quartzite

The appearance of marble with the durability of granite. Formed under intense heat and pressure, quartzite is one of the hardest natural stones — naturally resistant to etching and low porosity.

04
Limestone

Warm, muted tones with a naturally aged quality. Excellent for flooring, wall cladding, and outdoor applications where a softer, more organic aesthetic is desired.

05
Soapstone

Non-porous, heat-resistant, and stain-proof without sealing. Develops a rich natural patina over time. Never needs sealing — a surface that genuinely improves with age.

Never Needs Sealing
06
Quartz

Engineered for consistency and performance. The ideal solution where uniformity, color matching, and zero maintenance are priorities — particularly for large commercial installations.

How It Works

From Slab to Surface

Step 01
Slab Selection

Visit our slab yard and select your stone in person. Our team helps you understand grain, color, and veining so you choose with confidence.

Step 02
Expert Templating

Our template measurer, with 23 years of hands-on experience at Stonecraft, takes precise, on-site measurements at your jobsite before a single cut is made.

Step 03
In-House Fabrication

Every slab is cut, edged, and finished in our Knoxville fabrication plant by experienced hands.

Step 04
Professional Installation

Our installation team handles delivery and setting with guaranteed workmanship. We don't leave until it's right.
